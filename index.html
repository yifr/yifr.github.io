About Me

Hi! My name is Yoni, and I'm currently working as a research engineer in MIT's Computational Cognitive Science Lab. I hope to start my PhD in the fall of 2023, working on building better models of both human and machine scene understanding. I'm particularly excited about gestalt grouping, and the beautiful mechanisms that allow our minds to fill in the gaps in our visual input.
